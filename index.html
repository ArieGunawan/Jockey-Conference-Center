The Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a leading event venue in Jakarta, Indonesia. With its modern facilities and prime location, JCC hosts conferences, exhibitions, and cultural events. It offers versatile spaces, advanced technology, and exceptional services. JCC's professional team ensures seamless event execution, making it a preferred choice for both local and international organizers. From intimate meetings to large-scale conventions, JCC provides a conducive environment for successful events.
