Why Us?

The JCC is a notable convention center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. The JCC is one of Jakarta's oldest and most well-known conference venues The Asian-African Conference and the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference are only two of the notable national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor athletic events, and musical concerts that JCC has hosted since it first opened its doors in 1974. All year long, it is a very popular destination.
